Getting to Base Camp

November 23, 2022
1 minute read

Twelve months ago I began my Ascent. Twelve months ago I resigned from my job to try and become a Bitcoin Core contributor.

1415 working hours later and it’s time for a checkpoint.

When you decide to ascent a mountain, especially on any big expedition, you never go directly to the top. You start by hiking through the foothills, until you reach base camp. Base Camp is the first camp you setup at the base of the mountain which you want to summit.

Basecamp is a checkpoint, a moment of reflection. An opportunity to look back, contemplate your mistakes, celebrate your wins, and reassess the map of your journey.
